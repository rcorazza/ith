Welcome...

J & J Sales & Marketing, Inc is a Manufacturers’ Representative of Commercial and Industrial Pipe, Valves & Fittings products.  Our products are sold to the Wholesale Distributors in the Chicago-land & Midwest Markets.

J & J Sales has been performing these sales and marketing functions since 1990 and we have 90 years of combined sales experience in the Pipe, Valve & Fitting market.

We have many years of experience in the complex industries of Oil & Gas, Refinery, Steel, Chemical and Petrochemical markets.  In addition, we offer many products that are widely used in the commercial plumbing market.

This experience has given us a level of expertise that has allowed us to be a source of knowledge to our distributors as well as the end users of our products.

New for 2017/2018:

ABZ (Forum):

ABZ is a premier manufacturer of Butterfly Valves. They offer one of the most diversified lines of Butterfly Valves through 72”. Including general purpose valves with a wide range of seating and trim materials. Their offering includes valves for the Chemical, Food, Sanitary, Oil & Gas and Power industries. Their high performance butterfly valves are offered with both soft and metal seats.

If you are looking for complete automated packages, look no further than ABZ. They offer a complete line of pneumatic and electric actuators along with the accessories required for quick turn around.

DSI (Forum):

DSI is a full line manufacturer of Gate, Globe and Check Valves. With size ranges to 56" and pressure classes to Class 4500. Materials include CI, DI, CS, SS and low & high temp alloys. Forged Steel and Pressure Seals valves are also included in their offerings. DSI can provide API 624 low fugitive emission compliant valves and NACE certifications.
